Title:
My Stormy Tapestry

This poem depicts my struggle to come to grips with my life right now. Although I feel crushed with the amount of work and reponsibilites I have, I am relying on my friends and family to get me through!
Pieces of Us

From Peter

Grey clouds shadow the land below
The winds howl, the windows chatter
The rain falls, all life shivers

Black clouds darken the boy below
The fear corrupts, the heart clenches
The tears fall, the soul stills
