Title:
My Dusty Monitor

We all have that one thing we bought, yet have yet to use or open for months or even years. Although COVID-19 brought more misfortunes than fortunes, at least it provided me with enough incentives to finally open my dusty monitor.
Pieces of Us

From Annie





I bought a Dell monitor in freshman year of college using a one-time scholarship.






"Better use this money," I thought.


The monitor box sat in my basement for years,
never worth the effort of transporting it to school.



Until COVID-19 hit,
and I began all-remote classes.
As I stared at my 13" laptop screen every day,
I thought about my unopened monitor at home.




Surely, this was the time?
