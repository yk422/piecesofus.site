Title:
Identity

This short story depicts my ongoing internal struggle in figuring out what kind of person I am. Nonetheless, I believe that the process of self discovery is one of the most meaningful journeys that we take in our lives.
Pieces of Us

From Ally

The world is full of color.

If I had a color, I wonder what color I would be.

To be honest, I am scared that I might be colorless.

What do I love? What do I hate?
There are so many things about myself
that I don't know.

Who is the real me? Which is the true me?
