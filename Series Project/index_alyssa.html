Title:
The View from My Window

This is an exerpt from my daily journal. Although I cannot control many things in life, like how there is the large McCarter building preventing me from seeing the sky and the sunset, I will try to continue to find the good in every bad.
Pieces of Us

From Alyssa

It’s 5:19 right now.
The sun is setting.
The sky is baby blue with a warm orange ombre
at the toes of the sky.


I wish McCarter was not built in front of my windows
so that I could enjoy the sun setting.


It’s a great building and all but
the sunset is more beautiful.


I guess I can see the sunset anywhere else but
I can only see McCarter in person here at Princeton.
I’ll stop complaining about the view.
