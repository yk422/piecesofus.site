Title:
My Stormy Tapestry

This poem depicts my struggle to come to grips with my life right now. Although I feel crushed with the amount of work and reponsibilites I have, I am relying on my friends and family to get me through!
Pieces of Us

From Peter

But above the clouds is sun and blue
But beneath the tapestry are windows and clear
The storms will rage, this I know

But its duration, I do not
So I wait and endure and live
For sun to shine through windows
For the tapestry to rip.
